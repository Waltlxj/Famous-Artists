Data Source

Name: "Best Artworks of All Time: Collection of Paintings of the 50 Most Influential Artists of All Time"

Origin: The data was scraped from artchallenge.ru during the end of February 2019 and published on Kaggle by user "Icaro".

Summary: The data consists of a CSV file and a folder of 8355 images. The file contains information about 50 famous artists, and the folder contains examples of their artwork. For each artist, the file provides their full name, the years they were alive, their nationalities, the genres of art they contributed to, a short biography, and a link to their Wikipedia page.
